About the Swedish Chef

The Swedish Chef is a Muppet character from the sketch comedy television series The Muppet Show, known mainly for his eccentric culinary capabilities.

He was originally performed by Jim Henson and Frank Oz simultaneously, with Henson performing the head and voice in mock and Oz performing the character

with real hands. The Swedish Chef is currently performed by Bill Barretta. He is best known for his ridiculous cooking methods and the phrase "Bork, bork, bork!".
